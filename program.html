A Learning Experience Designed for Senior Leadership Impact

The OSB Executive MBA is a 15-month blended journey for senior executives navigating complexity and change. Delivered at the AUB Academic and Executive Hub in Dubai, it combines 17 residencies convening on Saturday and Sunday every three weeks with structured online learning, fostering strategic thinking and practical decision-making rooted in real business challenges.

The experience is enhanced by an international residency at a global partner business school, broadening participants’ exposure to global best practices and market perspectives, and concludes with a capstone project addressing real organizational or market challenges.
