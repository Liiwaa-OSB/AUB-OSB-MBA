Where an AUB MBA takes you.

The impact of the MBA is best seen in the journeys of its alumni — the outcomes they achieve and the growth they experience.

Career development

A degree that moves your career forward

Whether you want to advance, switch fields, or strengthen your leadership, the MBA is built to get you there. Applied projects, leadership workshops, and strong employer connections translate directly into the roles graduates go on to hold.

AUB ranks 33rd worldwide for employability and alumni outcomes and 3rd in MENA for employability — reinforcing its standing as a launchpad for ambitious professionals.

Employment profile

Most students are employed full-time. The leading industry is Telecommunications, followed by Construction, Financial Services, and Healthcare — with graduates also building careers in technology, consulting, and consumer goods.

Support

CMS & the Career Readiness Program

Career Management Services (CMS) guides you throughout the MBA — advising, placement support, and skill-building. In partnership with the Career Readiness Program (CRP), full-time students can spend a semester working in a leading company, gaining experience that opens doors to future growth.
