Workshops

Beyond the syllabus

Zero-credit workshops focused on career planning, communication, and high-performance leadership — practical, hands-on, and immediately applicable.

i

Career Strategic Planning

A five-hour interactive session giving you the mindset and tools to design a purposeful, sustainable career — exploring strengths, assessing the market, and building employability skills from profile to networking.

ii

Communication Skills

Learn from an expert how to communicate effectively in business. A hands-on workshop bridging knowledge and practice across written, visual, and oral communication.

iii

The Human Factor in Business Performance

How behavior, communication, and judgment shape performance under pressure — drawing on applied psychology and neuroscience to help you lead and decide effectively in high-stakes settings.

Networking

Connections that outlast the degree

The MBA Talking Series is an interactive set of nine sessions where alumni share insights on relevant industry topics — practical perspectives and real-world experience that broaden your understanding of today's business challenges.
