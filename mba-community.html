Student life at OSB extends well beyond the classroom — a holistic experience that supports both personal and professional growth.

Life as an MBA student

Grow in the room, and everywhere around it

From day one you're part of a diverse, dynamic cohort spanning a wide range of professional backgrounds, nationalities, and levels of experience. That diversity creates a collaborative environment where perspectives are shared, challenged, and expanded — enriching the whole academic and professional journey.

Alongside your studies you'll find networking, workshops, clubs, centers, and curated events, plus the support services that help you make the most of it all.

Support

Career Management Services

Through CMS at OSB, students receive continuous guidance throughout their MBA journey — from career advising and consultation to job-placement support and skill-building sessions — helping you navigate your path with confidence and clarity.

Career advising

One-to-one consultation to shape a purposeful, sustainable path.

Placement support

Connections and guidance to reach the right opportunities.

Skill-building

Sessions that sharpen the capabilities employers look for.

Workshops

Beyond the syllabus

Zero-credit workshops focused on career planning, communication, and high-performance leadership — practical, hands-on, and immediately applicable.

i

Career Strategic Planning

A five-hour interactive session giving you the mindset and tools to design a purposeful, sustainable career — exploring strengths, assessing the market, and building employability skills from profile to networking.

ii

Communication Skills

Learn from an expert how to communicate effectively in business. A hands-on workshop bridging knowledge and practice across written, visual, and oral communication.

iii

The Human Factor in Business Performance

How behavior, communication, and judgment shape performance under pressure — drawing on applied psychology and neuroscience to help you lead and decide effectively in high-stakes settings.

Networking

Connections that outlast the degree

The MBA Talking Series is an interactive set of nine sessions where alumni share insights on relevant industry topics — practical perspectives and real-world experience that broaden your understanding of today's business challenges.

Add the international study trip, OSB's clubs and centers, and curated events, and you build a network that spans industries and borders.
