Why AUB OSB

Building the future of business leadership

At the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business, the MBA develops forward-thinking leaders ready to shape the future of business. Blending academic rigor with real-world application, the program builds critical thinking, strategic insight, and the leadership capabilities to navigate a fast-changing world.

Established in the mid-1940s as the first MBA launched in Lebanon, the Middle East, and Europe, the program remains #1 in the MENA region in the QS MBA ranking — reinforcing its position as a leading choice for ambitious professionals.

World-class rigor paired with the networks and relevance of the region you'll lead in.

ii

Consulting projects, leadership labs, and participatory classrooms that turn theory into practice.

Questions

Frequently asked

Can I study while working?
Yes. The program offers both part-time and full-time enrollment, with in-person afternoon classes designed to accommodate working professionals as well as full-time students.
When can I start?
You can begin in either Fall or Spring. Fall begins in late August, while Spring starts in early to mid January.
How long does the MBA take?
The MBA typically takes 12–18 months depending on your intake and whether you pursue the program full-time or part-time.
Do I need a GMAT or GRE?
Admissions requirements should be confirmed with the MBA team. If you plan to include a GMAT or GRE policy here, link it directly to the official admissions details page.
What language is the program taught in?
All instruction is delivered in English.
