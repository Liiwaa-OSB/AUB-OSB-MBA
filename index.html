Why AUB OSB

Building the future of business leadership

At the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business, the MBA develops forward-thinking leaders ready to shape the future of business. Blending academic rigor with real-world application, the program builds critical thinking, strategic insight, and the leadership capabilities to navigate a fast-changing world.

Established in the mid-1940s as the first MBA launched in Lebanon, the Middle East, and Europe, the program remains #1 in the MENA region in the QS MBA ranking, reinforcing its position as a leading choice for ambitious professionals.

i

A global perspective, rooted regionally

World-class rigor paired with the networks and relevance of the region you'll lead in.

ii

Learning by doing

Consulting projects, leadership labs, and participatory classrooms that turn theory into practice.

iii

Built around your career

Full-time or part-time enrollment designed for working professionals and full-time students alike.

iv

Doors that open worldwide

International study trips, an NYU semester exchange, and a Career Readiness placement semester.

The Program

A curriculum engineered for impact and flexibility

A strong, well-structured program that accommodates both full-time students and working professionals, each course designed to let you learn, apply, and lead.

Mode of study

Full-time or part-time

Study intensively or alongside your career.

Format

In-person

Afternoon classes on the AUB campus.

Intakes

Fall & Spring

Fall begins late August; Spring in early–mid January.

Duration

12–18 months

Fall: 4 terms full-time / 6 part-time. Spring: 5 / 6.

Language

English

All instruction delivered in English.

Ideal candidate

2+ years' experience

Professionals, entrepreneurs, and aspiring leaders ready to advance or pivot.
