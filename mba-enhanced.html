Program Overview

Reshape your future in 16 months

At the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business at AUB, the MBA develops forward-thinking leaders ready to shape the future of business. Blending academic rigor with real-world application, the program builds critical thinking, strategic insight, and leadership capabilities to navigate a fast-changing world.

Ranked #1 in the MENA region (QS), it offers flexible full-time and part-time formats, enriched by experiential learning, strong employer connections, and international exposure.

Full-time (16 months) · Part-time (24 months)
Bilingual options (English / Arabic tracks)
4+ global residencies included

FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the OSB MBA journey.

What are the admission requirements?
Bachelor's degree, GMAT/GRE (waiver available for qualified professionals), English proficiency, 2+ years work experience recommended. Holistic review.
Is the MBA available part-time?
Yes, a flexible part-time format designed for working professionals, with evening & weekend classes and the same world-class curriculum.
What scholarships are offered?
Merit-based scholarships, diversity fellowships, and corporate sponsorship opportunities — with up to 100% tuition coverage available.
Do you offer career support?
Dedicated career development center, 1:1 coaching, corporate treks, internship placements, and lifetime alumni access.
