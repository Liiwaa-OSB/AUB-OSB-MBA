A Diverse and Experienced Cohort

The OSB Executive MBA attracts experienced leaders with a track record of impact and growth. Participants typically bring 8 or more years of professional experience, a mix of managerial and leadership roles, and diverse educational backgrounds.

Our cohorts span industries from finance and consulting to technology, healthcare, and beyond. The mix of backgrounds and experiences comes together to form a highly engaged, collaborative community where diverse perspectives and real-world expertise drive every discussion.
