Eligibility

Admissions criteria

i

Bachelor's degree

From a university recognized by AUB, with a good GPA.

ii

GPA ≈ 80 (≈ 3.2–3.3)

Or its standardized equivalent from a recognized university.

iii

GMAT or GRE

Required for most applicants.

iv

2+ years' experience

At least two years of relevant work experience.

How it works

The application process

Start with a short form and our recruiters will reach out to guide you through the requirements and the steps below.

1

Start an online application

Begin your application and connect with the MBA recruitment team.

2

Complete it through AUB's official portal

Submit your full application via the university's admissions portal.

3

Upload your documents

Transcripts, degrees, CV/résumé, statement of purpose, recommendation letters, and language test (if available).

4

Submit test scores

GMAT or GRE, plus English proficiency (TOEFL / IELTS / Duolingo, etc.).

5

Application review

Your file is assessed holistically against the admissions criteria.

6

Interview (if required)

A conversation to learn more about your goals and fit.

7

Final decision

You receive your admissions outcome.
