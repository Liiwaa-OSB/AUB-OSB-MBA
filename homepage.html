Program overview — reshape your future

At the Suliman S. Olayan School of Business at American University of Beirut, the MBA develops forward-thinking leaders ready to shape the future of business. Blending academic rigor with real-world application, the program builds critical thinking, strategic insight, and leadership capabilities to navigate a fast-changing world. Ranked #1 in the MENA region (QS), it offers flexible full-time and part-time formats, enriched by experiential learning, strong employer connections, and international exposure, equipping graduates to lead with impact both regionally and globally.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the OSB MBA journey.

What are the admission requirements?
Bachelor’s degree, GMAT/GRE (waiver available for qualified professionals), English proficiency, 2+ years work experience recommended. Holistic review.
Is the MBA available part-time?
Yes, flexible part-time format designed for working professionals, evening & weekend classes, same world-class curriculum.
What scholarships are offered?
Merit-based scholarships, diversity fellowships, corporate sponsorship opportunities. Up to 100% tuition coverage.
Do you offer career support?
Dedicated career development center, 1:1 coaching, corporate treks, internship placements, and lifetime alumni access.
